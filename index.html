About Us

Engineering practical handling solutions for modern industry.


We are an Australian manufacturer specializing in forklift attachments designed for safe and efficient handling of trailers, campers, and vehicles.

Our solutions are engineered to simplify material handling challenges across manufacturing facilities, logistics yards, and storage environments.
